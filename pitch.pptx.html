01
The problem

The route has to be right before speech.

Voice agents already choose a path before the reply is spoken. The hard part is knowing which path is safe.

Answer

Fast when safe

Stable product and navigation questions should speak immediately without tool delay.

Verify

Check live facts

Account, billing, policy, and availability questions need RAG, tools, or backend data.

Escalate

Hand off early

Fraud, legal, urgent, and angry-customer turns should reach a human before improvisation.

02
The insight

Same words. Different action.

One topic, three correct responses. The right move depends on risk, not the keyword.

Answer
"Where is your refund policy?"
Stable public info. Speak instantly, no lookup.
Verify
"Am I eligible for a refund?"
Depends on this account. Check the data, then speak.
Escalate
"Someone used my card without permission."
Risk and fraud. Hand to a human, do not improvise.
The topic is similar. The correct route depends on operational risk, not just intent.
